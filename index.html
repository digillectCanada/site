About Digillect

Reliability Is an Engineering Problem.

SRE Cloud Architecture Kubernetes Observability Cloud Migration Platform Engineering Incident Response Disaster Recovery AWS GitOps GitLab CI Dagger
"Reliability isn't a feature you bolt on — it's a discipline you build in from the ground up."

Digillect is a boutique SRE consultancy based in downtown Toronto, founded by a senior cloud architect and site reliability engineer with over a decade of experience scaling distributed systems at enterprise scale.

We work with engineering teams that are serious about availability — organizations growing past the point where informal practices hold, and platforms where an outage isn't just inconvenient, it's costly. We bring enterprise-grade SRE methodology without the enterprise overhead.

Whether you're migrating workloads to the cloud, building a production-grade Kubernetes platform, hardening your disaster recovery posture, or designing the observability layer that will carry you into your next growth phase — we've done it, and we can help you get there faster.

What We Do

Services

Every engagement is shaped around your team's actual needs — not a playbook. We embed with your engineers, understand your constraints, and deliver work that sticks long after the engagement ends.

// 01
SRE Consultancy & Embed

End-to-end SRE advisory for teams building reliability from scratch or maturing existing practices. We assess your error budget framework, SLO/SLA definitions, toil reduction opportunities, and runbook quality — then work directly with your engineers to close the gaps.

SLOs / SLAs Error Budgets Toil Reduction Runbooks On-Call Design
// 02
Observability & Monitoring

Full-stack observability using Prometheus, Grafana, OpenTelemetry, and Datadog — custom dashboards, alerting hierarchies, distributed tracing, and log aggregation pipelines. Your on-call engineer will wake up knowing exactly what broke and why.

Prometheus Grafana OpenTelemetry Datadog Alerting Design Tracing
// 03
Kubernetes — Full Spectrum

Complete Kubernetes expertise at every layer. Cluster architecture, multi-tenancy, workload hardening, autoscaling, service mesh with Istio and Cilium, GitOps with Argo CD, Helm, Crossplane for infrastructure-as-CRDs, Velero backup and restore, cost optimization, RBAC — the full ecosystem.

EKS / GKE / AKS Istio Cilium Argo CD Helm Crossplane Velero HPA / KEDA
// 04
CI/CD & Platform Reliability

Delivery pipelines built on GitLab CI and Dagger — fast, safe, and fully reproducible. Dagger gives you portable, testable pipeline logic that runs identically locally and in CI. Canary releases, blue-green deployments, and progressive delivery baked in. Terraform for IaC and drift detection.

GitLab CI Dagger Terraform Canary Deploys Blue-Green GitOps
// 05
Cloud Migration & DR

Lift-and-shift is the floor, not the ceiling. We design cloud-native migration strategies for workloads moving to AWS — re-platforming to Kubernetes, database migration, stateful workload handling. Plus disaster recovery postures with defined RTO/RPO targets, multi-region failover, backup automation, and DR runbooks that actually get exercised.

AWS Migration Re-Platforming RTO / RPO Multi-Region DR Runbooks Backup Automation
// 06
Incident Response Design

On-call shouldn't mean dread. We redesign incident response from alert routing through to postmortem culture — PagerDuty schedule design, escalation policies, blameless postmortem processes, and tabletop exercises run with your team. You'll finish with the tooling and confidence to handle incidents with clarity.

PagerDuty Escalation Policy Blameless Postmortems Tabletop Exercises
